How it Works

Knack at Western is a workshop to help students gain experience teaching everyday subjects to absolute beginners, and help those beginners walk away with a tangible new skill.


We want to create a 'maker' culture at Western and provide a learning experience unlike anything you can find in a classroom.

We've Broken It Down into Two Parts


Learn It.

If you're looking to learn a new, tangible skill that's outside of something could learn in any class, come to Knack's very first event! We will be hosting a variety of skill workshops including game development, tea brewing, juggling and even DJing.

The cost to attend the event is $4, and will include full acess to all the workshops (including a brakfast workishop) and lunch!

Oh yeah, and the first 50 people people to sign up on our Tilt get entered into a draw for a $25 gift card from Hospitality Services (that's like 14 coffees!).

Sign Up Now!

Teach It.

This workshop is run by students for students. It consists of sessions running anywhere from one to two hours. Your audience will get hands-on training for a new skill. Teaching at Knack at Western will give you first-hand exposure to running workshops, public speaking, but most importantly, conveying new concepts and skills to your cohorts. We will provide the space, time, audience and even materials needed for your workshop session. Your only job is to make sure that you leave your peers with a new skill and have fun sharing your knowledge.

Frequently Asked Questions


What can I teach?
You can teach any skill that you think is cool. We know that's general so here are some examples:

How to program a quick game

How to juggle

How to knit

How to do stand-up comedy

How to color (I still struggle to stay inside the lines)


I'm not sure if I can teach _________.
Most likely the answer is yes! The most import thing is for you to apply! You can also message us on our Facebook Page and we can guide you in the right direction!

Can I also attend other workshops if I'm teaching one?
Yes, absolutely! In fact, we encourage this. Ideally everyone who runs workshops will also attend others.

Will there be food?
Yup. We'll be providing you with a lunch during the event. If you have any dietary restrictions it's important that you fill out our Learn It. form.

How long are each session?
Each session will be between 30 minutes and two hours.

Do I have to participate in every workshop?
No, however we encourage you to step outside of your comfort zone and try something new :).

Where is it?
Knack at Western will be hosted in Claudette MacKay-Lassonde Pavilion (CMLP) and Spencer Engineering Building (SEB). Specific rooms will be announced soon!

What should I bring?

If you are running a workshop, you should bring the basic materials that you need for your workshop. We can provide you with some of the things that you need for your workshop, however. All of this can be worked out in our application form

If you are attending a workshop, we will provide a list of anything you will need to bring for each workshop. For example, for a yoga workshop bring a yoga mat!
